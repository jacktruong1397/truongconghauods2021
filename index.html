ABOUT US

The La COMÉ Cafe was founded in Tay Ninh Province Vietnam in 2019 by Thanh Nguyen and The co-founder is Truong Cong Hau. The two young guys decided to start the very first business which is blowing the very fresh wind to the FB industry in their hometown. We are serving comfortable seats, amazed decorations and diversity beverages.

The menu always updates during seasons. We have the best coffee in town and we serve freash made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches.
